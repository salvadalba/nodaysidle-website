About NDI

Company Story

NDI began as a two‑person engineering effort to make PCs and workstations behave like dependable tools, not toys. We focus on latency, reliability, and throughput under real workloads. “No Days Idle” reflects our commitment to systems that keep producing, 24/7.

From bespoke thermal designs to workload‑specific pipelines (CAD/3D/ML/video), our approach is diagnostic‑first, data‑driven, and pragmatic.
